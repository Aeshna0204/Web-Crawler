Description

It’s Stunning, Smart & Superior- MagicBricks iPhone app.

MagicBricks iPhone app is an intelligently designed Real Estate app for smart users who are looking for an interesting, intuitive and feature packed app for buying, selling and renting properties on the Go!

Simple, clean yet visually appealing, this Real Estate iPhone app is designed to fulfil your residential & commercial property requirement in a very simple and fast way.

Our cleverly designed app helps you refine your property search with the help of filters like BHK, budget, furnished, possession date etc so that you get your dream property easily & quickly. Not only this, our smart app displays properties in the form of list, photos & map to give a comprehensive view of the property and make your property selection easier & quicker.

Searching for flats for sale or rent in a particular locality was never this easy. You can also check locality reviews, locality ratings and compare various localities for buying or renting purpose on our Locality Review section. Our app allows you to stay up-to-date with the latest price trends in the property market with our Price Trends feature.

Our next gen app is designed to equip you with posting a property effortlessly. In fact we have made it so easy that you can even take photos through the app directly and post it with your listings instantly.

If you are one of those who prefer taking the help of an agent to buy, sell or rent a property, our iOS app allows you to search and contact an agent conveniently anytime and anywhere. You can shortlist agents, add them as favourites and contact them later.

Magicbricks Free iPhone App is:
-- Convenient: Search Properties & New Projects on the move; set property alerts & get matching properties for sale or rent in your inbox
-- Feature packed: Compare property price trends, view neighborhood ratings, photos & reviews.
-- Smart: Post your property even ‘On The Go’. Checkout responses received on your property & contact the buyers instantly.

Key Features:
> Choose from over 6 lac properties
> Refine your search with the help of filters like price, bedrooms, locality, etc
> Set property alerts & get matching properties directly in your inbox
> Contact Advertisers on the go. Call, Send SMS/email to property advertisers for FREE
> Search agents/property dealers on the basis of properties, locality and city they are dealing in
> Save properties, projects and agents for future reference
> Post property & showcase it over 15 lac buyers
> Check out price trends to evaluate property rental & capital values
> Check out the latest news/happenings in the real estate sector
> View & manage properties posted & checkout the responses received through My Magicbox

About MagicBricks:
MagicBricks.com is India’s #1 Real Estate site which offers you to buy, sell, rent all kind of residential and commercial properties in India. It offers 800,000 property listings across various cities in India. MagicBricks.com features a host of services, like Property Advice, Localities, Rates & Trends, Prop Index, Buyers Guide etc to help its users make wise property buying, selling & renting decisions.
